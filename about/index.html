About

I am a Junior Undergraduate in Electrical Engineering, IIT Kanpur. I am also pursuing a minor in Computer Science, specifically, Artificial Intelligence. I am broadly interested in Machine Learning, more specifically, Deep Learning and its applications in Computer Vision. I also regularly take part in competitive programming contests. Currently, I am working on the MovieQA problem under Dr. Vinay Namboodiri.

I hail from the holy city of Amritsar, Punjab. Just like many other math oriented students in India, I decided to give the “JEE” in 2014. I did decently well, securing AIR 376 after two years of toil, which landed me in Electrical Engineering at IIT Kanpur. Why Electrical @ IIT Kanpur? That constitutes a separate discussion. It suffices to say though that Electrical Engineering has been more than gratifying for me as an undergraduate.

Freshmen year was my first formal tryst with programming, to which I have been hooked to ever since. I started competitive programming shortly after. I recently qualified for ACM ICPC Regionals in Amritapuri and Chennai, securing 63 rank in the online qualification round.

Summer of 2016, I got to intern in Texas A&M University, under Dr. Srinivas Shakkottai. We worked on analyzing the privacy of a user in a DSRC environment, which enables vehicle to vehicle/infrastructure communication, using a combination of analytical and machine learning tools.

For more details pertaining to my academics and projects, please refer to my CV.
